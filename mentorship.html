How the Mentorship Program Works

We seamlessly match learners, mentors, volunteers, and partners using a data-driven matching system.

Step 1 - Apply

Learners, mentors, volunteers, and partners submit applications through the platform.

Step 2 - Match

Participants are matched based on interests, goals, experience, and mentorship tracks.

Step 3 - Learn

Mentors guide mentees through structured sessions, growth plans, and learning pathways.

Step 4 - Grow

Participants gain access to workshops, networking, internships, and volunteer tracks.

Mentor Responsibilities

What we expect from our mentors

✓ Regular Check-Ins

Meet with your mentees at least twice monthly (video or in-person)

✓ Responsive Communication

Respond to mentee messages within 48 hours

✓ Career Guidance

Provide advice on career paths, job search, and professional development

✓ Skill Development

Share industry insights and practical knowledge from your experience

✓ Goal Setting

Help mentees set and work towards realistic, achievable goals

✓ Project Support

Guide mentees through their capstone projects and assignments

✓ Networking Support

Introduce mentees to relevant contacts when appropriate

✓ Feedback & Encouragement

Provide constructive feedback and moral support throughout

Time Commitment: Approximately 3-4 hours per month (varies by mentee needs)
