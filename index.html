Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our program

We welcome applications from young Africans passionate about digital growth, including students, recent graduates, and career switchers looking to enter the tech space.
The program is an intensive 8-week structured journey designed to build your skills week by week.
Please refer to the application page for specific details regarding program costs and any available scholarship opportunities.
You will receive one-on-one mentorship, access to expert-led training sessions, project-based learning, and career pathway mapping.
Matching is based on your specific career goals, interests, and the expertise of our mentors to ensure a productive and personalized experience.
