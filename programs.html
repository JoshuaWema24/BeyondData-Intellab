The BeyondData Intellab Accelerator

An 8-Week Digital Career Accelerator designed to prepare young Africans for the digital economy, employability opportunities, entrepreneurship, and technology-driven careers.

Cohort-Based Learning Experience

We run in structured cohorts to ensure personalized mentorship, accountability, collaboration, and measurable growth outcomes.

How the 8-Week Accelerator Works

Week 1

Orientation & Digital Foundations

Introduction to the program, digital economy awareness, productivity tools, communication, and personal growth planning.

Week 2

Data & Digital Literacy

Data basics, digital workflows, spreadsheets, internet research, and foundational analytical thinking.

Week 3

AI, Automation & Modern Work Tools

Practical exposure to AI tools, automation systems, responsible AI usage, and digital productivity enhancement.

Week 4

Personal Branding & Visibility

LinkedIn optimization, CV improvement, online visibility, digital identity, and portfolio foundations.

Week 5

Career & Employability Readiness

Interview preparation, remote work readiness, freelancing exposure, communication skills, and workplace professionalism.

Week 6

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

Digital business systems, innovation thinking, problem solving, and entrepreneurship fundamentals.

Week 7

Project Development

Collaborative assignments, capstone preparation, mentorship reviews, and portfolio building.

Week 8

Showcase & Pathways Forward

Final presentations, assessments, mentorship reflections, networking, internship pathways, volunteer opportunities, and next-step planning.
